50 Best ChatGPT Prompts for Business in 2026 (Copy & Paste)

You don't need to be a prompt engineer. You just need the right prompts. These 50 have been tested across real businesses — copy, paste, swap in your details, and go.

Sales & Outreach (10 Prompts)

1. Cold email that gets replies

"Write a cold email to [job title] at [type of company]. Our product does [what it does]. Keep it under 100 words. Open with a pain point, not a compliment. End with a soft CTA."

2. Follow-up after no reply

"Write a short follow-up email to [name] who hasn't responded to my original email about [topic]. Be friendly, add one new piece of value, and keep it under 60 words."

3. Sales page headline variations

"Write 10 headline variations for a sales page selling [product]. Target audience: [audience]. Each headline should use a different angle: fear, aspiration, curiosity, specificity, social proof."

4. Objection handler

"A prospect said '[objection]'. Write 3 responses I could give that acknowledge their concern, reframe it, and guide them back toward a yes."

5. Discovery call questions

"I'm about to get on a discovery call with [type of prospect]. Write 8 open-ended questions to understand their pain points, budget, timeline, and decision-making process."

6. Proposal executive summary

"Write an executive summary for a proposal offering [service] to [type of client]. Budget: [X]. Their main goal: [goal]. Max 150 words. Professional, outcome-focused."

7. LinkedIn connection request message

"Write a LinkedIn connection request message to [role] at [company type]. Reference [shared interest/context]. Keep under 300 characters."

8. Upsell script

"Write a short upsell script for telling an existing customer about [upgraded product/service]. They already bought [original]. Keep it conversational and value-focused."

9. Re-engagement email for cold leads

"Write a re-engagement email to leads who went cold 3 months ago. We sell [product]. Don't mention the gap — just offer something new or a limited reason to act now."

10. Proposal closing paragraph

"Write a closing paragraph for a business proposal that creates urgency, reminds the prospect of the ROI, and makes the next step (signing) feel easy and obvious."

Marketing & Content (10 Prompts)

11. Blog post outline

"Create a detailed outline for a blog post titled '[title]'. Include intro hook, 5-7 H2 sections with bullet points, and a conclusion with CTA. Target keyword: [keyword]."

12. Email newsletter in one shot

"Write a weekly email newsletter for [business type]. Topic this week: [topic]. Format: short intro, 3 tips with brief explanations, one CTA. Tone: conversational and direct."

13. YouTube video script (short-form)

"Write a 60-second YouTube script for [topic]. Hook in first 3 seconds. Core value in the middle. End with a direct CTA. No filler words."

14. Instagram caption with hook

"Write an Instagram caption for [content type/image description]. Include a scroll-stopping first line, 3-5 value sentences, and a question to drive comments. Add 5 hashtags."

15. Case study paragraph

"Write a case study paragraph about a client who used [product/service] and got [result]. Format: situation → solution → outcome. Keep it concrete with numbers."

16. Ad copy (Meta/Facebook)

"Write a Facebook ad for [product]. Target: [audience]. Pain point: [pain]. Format: hook (1 line), problem (2 lines), solution (2 lines), proof (1 line), CTA (1 line). Under 125 words."

17. Product description

"Write a product description for [product]. Features: [list]. Target buyer: [audience]. Focus on benefits over features. End with a confident CTA. Under 100 words."

18. Testimonial request email

"Write an email asking a happy client to leave a review. They had a great experience with [what you did]. Keep it friendly, make it easy — offer a template or specific questions."

19. SEO meta description

"Write a meta description for a page about [topic/page title]. Target keyword: [keyword]. Include the keyword naturally, communicate value, and stay under 155 characters."

20. Content repurposing prompt

"Here's a blog post: [paste]. Turn it into: 3 tweets, 1 LinkedIn post, 1 email, and 5 short video hooks. Keep each platform's native tone."

Operations & Productivity (10 Prompts)

21. SOPs from scratch

"Write a step-by-step SOP for [process]. Audience: new team member with no prior experience. Format: numbered steps, clear actions, no jargon. Include what to do if something goes wrong."

22. Meeting agenda

"Create a 45-minute meeting agenda for [topic]. Include time blocks, discussion points, owner for each item, and a clear decision to be made by the end."

23. Job description

"Write a job description for a [role] at [type of company]. Include responsibilities, requirements, nice-to-haves, and a short company description. Tone: professional but human."

24. Performance review template

"Create a structured performance review template for a [role]. Include sections for achievements, areas of improvement, goal setting, and a rating scale. Professional tone."

25. Project brief

"Write a project brief for [project name]. Include: objective, scope, deliverables, timeline, stakeholders, success metrics, and risks. Keep it concise — one page max."

26. Weekly team update email

"Write a weekly update email from a team lead to their manager covering the week of [date]. Include: what was completed, what's in progress, blockers, and next week's priorities."

27. Onboarding checklist

"Create a 30-60-90 day onboarding checklist for a new [role]. Day 1-30: learning. Day 31-60: contributing. Day 61-90: owning. Format as a checklist with checkboxes."

28. KPI dashboard description

"Write a description of 5 key KPIs for a [type of business]. For each: what it measures, why it matters, how to calculate it, and what a good benchmark looks like."

29. Client onboarding email

"Write a welcome email to send to a new client who just signed up for [service]. Include: what happens next, their point of contact, timeline, and a warm but professional tone."

30. Process improvement suggestion

"Analyze this workflow: [describe workflow]. Identify 3 bottlenecks and suggest improvements for each. Focus on saving time without adding complexity."

Finance & Strategy (10 Prompts)

31. Pricing page copy

"Write copy for a 3-tier pricing page for [product/service]. Tiers: [names and prices]. Make the middle tier feel like the obvious choice. Each tier: 1 headline, 4 bullets, CTA."

32. Investor one-pager

"Write a one-pager for investors about [company]. Include: problem, solution, market size, traction, business model, team, and ask. Professional and punchy."

33. Financial model assumptions

"List 10 key assumptions I should define before building a financial model for a [type of business]. For each, explain why it matters and how to estimate it."

34. Revenue forecast narrative

"Write a 1-paragraph narrative explaining the revenue forecast for [company] for the next 12 months. Assumptions: [list]. Tone: confident but honest."

35. SWOT analysis

"Run a SWOT analysis for a [type of business] with these characteristics: [describe]. Format as a 2x2 grid with 3-4 bullet points per quadrant."

Customer Success (5 Prompts)

36. Complaint response

"Write a response to this customer complaint: '[paste complaint]'. Acknowledge the issue, apologize without over-apologizing, explain what you'll do, and offer a resolution."

37. FAQ page

"Generate 10 FAQ questions and answers for a [type of business] selling [product/service]. Address: pricing, delivery, refunds, how it works, and common objections."

38. Support ticket response

"Write a professional support response to this ticket: '[paste ticket]'. Be clear, helpful, and keep it under 100 words. End with an offer to help further."

39. Renewal reminder email

"Write a subscription renewal reminder email for [product]. Sending to [segment]. Remind them of value they've gotten, what they'd lose, and make renewing feel effortless."

40. Churn exit survey

"Write a 5-question survey to send to customers who just cancelled. Goal: understand why they left. Keep questions simple, mix multiple choice and open-ended."

AI Automation (10 Prompts)

41. Chatbot script for customer service

"Write a chatbot conversation script for [type of business]. Cover: greeting, FAQ handling, escalation to human, and lead capture. Include 5 example flows."

42. Automation blueprint

"Map out an automation workflow for [process]. Tools available: [list]. Show trigger → action → output for each step. Flag where human review is needed."

43. AI tool recommendation

"I run a [type of business] with [team size] people. Our biggest time waste is [pain point]. What AI tools would you recommend to solve this? For each: name, use case, price, and setup difficulty."

44. Prompt for training your own AI assistant

"Write a system prompt for a custom AI assistant for [business type]. It should know: [company info], our products/services, tone of voice, and how to handle common questions."

45. Data analysis request

"I have a spreadsheet with [describe data]. What patterns should I look for? Write the analysis steps, the questions to ask, and what charts to create."

46. AI-powered hiring screen

"Write a prompt to use with AI to screen CVs for a [role]. What criteria should the AI score? What should disqualify someone? Output: a scoring rubric."

47. Email triage system

"Design a system for AI to triage my email inbox. Categories: [list]. Rules for each category. Flag what needs my attention vs. what can be templated."

48. Content calendar builder

"Build a 4-week content calendar for [business type] on [platforms]. Include: topic, format, posting day, goal for each piece. Mix educational, promotional, and engagement content."

49. Competitive analysis

"Research prompt: analyze competitors of [company] in the [market]. For each competitor: strengths, weaknesses, pricing, positioning, and a gap we could exploit."

50. Business idea evaluation

"Evaluate this business idea: [describe]. Score it on: market size, competition, ease of entry, revenue model, and founder-market fit. Be honest about the risks."
