15 ChatGPT Prompts for Dropshipping That Turn Visitors Into Buyers

Dropshipping success comes down to finding winning products, driving targeted traffic, and converting that traffic with compelling copy. These prompts handle every copywriting challenge in your dropshipping funnel.

Prompt 1 of 15
Product page description (benefit-led)
Write a Shopify product page description for [PRODUCT NAME] priced at [PRICE]. Target customer: [AVATAR — e.g., dog owner, home organizer, fitness enthusiast]. Key features: [LIST 5]. Transform each feature into a direct customer benefit. Structure: hook sentence, 3-sentence value paragraph, 5 benefit bullets, sizing/spec table placeholder, and 'Why customers love it' section (social proof placeholder). Under 350 words.
💡 Pro tip: Dropshipping product pages with benefit bullets convert at 2-3x the rate of feature-only descriptions — the difference between 'waterproof' and 'keep hiking in any weather'.
Prompt 2 of 15
Facebook/Instagram video ad script (15-second)
Write a 15-second video ad script for [PRODUCT NAME] for Facebook/Instagram. Structure: 0-3 sec: problem/hook (show the pain). 3-10 sec: product in use (the transformation). 10-14 sec: social proof (star rating or customer count). 14-15 sec: CTA with offer. On-screen text at each section. No voiceover needed — text and visual driven.
💡 Pro tip: The hook in the first 3 seconds determines whether the video gets watched. Show the problem, not the product, in your opener.
Prompt 3 of 15
Facebook ad copy (cold audience)
Write 3 Facebook ad copy variations for [PRODUCT NAME] targeting [COLD AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION]. Each variation: Primary text (under 125 chars), Headline (under 27 chars), Description (under 27 chars). Variation 1: pain-point led. Variation 2: social proof led ('5,000+ happy customers'). Variation 3: urgency/scarcity led. Direct response tone.
💡 Pro tip: Test all 3 angles simultaneously with a $5/day budget per variant. The winner gets 80% of spend by day 5.
Prompt 4 of 15
Homepage hero copy (niche store)
Write hero section copy for the homepage of [STORE NAME], a [NICHE] Shopify store. Include: headline (benefit, under 10 words), sub-headline (who it's for + why it's better, under 20 words), primary CTA button text, and 3 trust badges (e.g., Free shipping, 30-day returns, 10,000+ customers). 3 headline variations to A/B test.
💡 Pro tip: Niche store homepages with a clear identity ('everything for remote workers') convert at 3x the rate of generic 'shop all products' messaging.
Prompt 5 of 15
Email: abandoned cart (3-sequence)
Write a 3-email abandoned cart sequence for a dropshipping store selling [PRODUCT CATEGORY]. Email 1 (1 hour): Short reminder, product image mention, back-to-cart link. Email 2 (24 hours): Add social proof (review quote) + address top objection ([COMMON OBJECTION]). Email 3 (72 hours): Discount offer ([X]% off, expires in 48 hours). Each under 150 words.
💡 Pro tip: The 3-email sequence recovers 15-20% of abandoned carts. Don't offer the discount until email 3 — you'll train customers to always abandon.
Prompt 6 of 15
Product review request email
Write a post-purchase review request email for [STORE NAME] sent [X] days after delivery of [PRODUCT]. Thank the customer, ask if they're enjoying the product, and invite them to leave a review with a direct link. Include a brief note about using their feedback to improve. Under 100 words. Genuine, not automated-feeling.
💡 Pro tip: Review request emails that read like they're from a real person (not an automated platform) get 3x more responses.
Prompt 7 of 15
TikTok ad script (30-second UGC-style)
Write a 30-second TikTok ad script for [PRODUCT NAME] in a UGC (user-generated content) style. Format: 0-5s hook ('POV: you finally found a [PRODUCT CATEGORY] that actually works'). 5-20s demo of the product solving the problem. 20-28s reaction/result. 28-30s CTA with offer. Conversational, authentic, not polished.
💡 Pro tip: TikTok ads that look and feel like organic content outperform polished ads by 4-5x. Imperfection is an asset.
Prompt 8 of 15
Upsell / cross-sell popup copy
Write copy for a post-purchase upsell popup offering [UPSELL PRODUCT] to customers who just bought [MAIN PRODUCT] at [STORE NAME]. Include: headline (benefit-led, under 10 words), 2-sentence explanation of why this product complements what they just bought, the offer ([DISCOUNT/BUNDLE DEAL]), and 2 button options: 'Add to order' and 'No thanks'. Under 100 words.
💡 Pro tip: Post-purchase upsell offers that explain the product pairing ('works even better with X') convert at 15-25% — the highest upsell conversion point in e-commerce.
Prompt 9 of 15
Influencer brief (for UGC creator)
Write a brief to send to a UGC content creator for [PRODUCT NAME]. Include: product description and key selling points (3), target audience, content format requested (15-30 second video showing [USE CASE]), what to say/show (3 specific talking points), what NOT to do (2-3 restrictions), and what they'll receive (free product + [FEE/COMMISSION]). Under 300 words.
💡 Pro tip: Detailed creator briefs produce better content on the first try — reducing revision rounds and time-to-ad.
Prompt 10 of 15
Winning product validation post (competitor research)
Help me write 5 Facebook search queries and 5 TikTok hashtag searches to validate that [PRODUCT NAME] is currently selling well. For Facebook: write search strings to find ads for this product. For TikTok: write hashtags that buyers of this product use. Then write a 'product validation checklist' of 5 signals that indicate a winning product.
💡 Pro tip: Validating a product through competitor ad research (FB Ad Library, TikTok search) takes 30 minutes and saves you from 3-month mistakes.
Prompt 11 of 15
Customer service response (late delivery complaint)
Write a response to a customer complaint about a late delivery for [STORE NAME]. The order was placed [X days ago] and hasn't arrived. Acknowledge the frustration, provide the current tracking status ([STATUS]), give a realistic arrival estimate, and offer a goodwill gesture ([DISCOUNT ON NEXT ORDER]). Under 150 words. Empathetic, accountable.
💡 Pro tip: Dropshipping's biggest weakness is shipping time. Proactive, empathetic service responses are your competitive advantage.
Prompt 12 of 15
Returns policy page copy
Write a clear, customer-friendly returns policy page for [STORE NAME]. Cover: what can be returned (conditions), the return window ([X days]), how to initiate a return (steps), refund processing time, shipping costs for returns, and what happens with defective items. Under 300 words. Plain English, no legal jargon. Builds trust with hesitant buyers.
💡 Pro tip: A clear, generous return policy increases conversion rate by 17% — it removes the risk of buying from an unknown store.
Prompt 13 of 15
Google Shopping product title
Write 5 Google Shopping product title variations for [PRODUCT NAME]. Include: brand name (if any), product type, key attributes (size, color, material, gender). Google Shopping title best practices: put the most important attributes first, use natural language, include high-search keywords ([KEYWORD LIST]). Max 150 characters each.
💡 Pro tip: Google Shopping titles with specific attributes (color, size, material) convert better than generic titles — shoppers are comparison shopping.
Prompt 14 of 15
Win-back email (lapsed buyer, 60+ days)
Write a win-back email for [STORE NAME] targeting customers who haven't purchased in 60+ days. Include: subject line that stands out ('We've missed you' alternatives), brief message acknowledging the time gap, a new product or collection they might like ([PRODUCT]), a limited-time offer ([DISCOUNT CODE: [CODE]]), and expiry date. Under 150 words.
💡 Pro tip: Win-back emails with a limited-time incentive recover 5-10% of lapsed customers — high ROI on a segment you'd otherwise lose forever.
Prompt 15 of 15
Store FAQ page (reduce support tickets)
Write a FAQ page for [STORE NAME], a [NICHE] dropshipping store. Answer these 6 common questions: (1) When will my order arrive? (2) Where do you ship from? (3) Can I return/exchange? (4) My order hasn't arrived — what do I do? (5) Are your products [quality/genuine/safe]? (6) How do I track my order? Honest, clear, under 100 words per answer.
💡 Pro tip: A good FAQ page reduces support emails by 30%. Answering 'where do you ship from?' honestly prevents the review 'shipped from China, took 3 weeks'.
