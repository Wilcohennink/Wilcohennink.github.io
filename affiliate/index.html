Frequently asked questions

How do I sign up?

Fill in the application form below. We ask for your name, email, website or social URL, estimated audience size, and how you plan to promote. We review all applications and reply within 24 hours.

When do I get paid?

On the 1st of each month for the previous month's confirmed earnings. For example, sales in April are paid on May 1st. We pay via PayPal or bank transfer (SEPA and international).

What is the cookie duration?

90 days. If a visitor clicks your affiliate link and purchases any time within 90 days, you receive the commission — even if they navigate away and come back later.

Can I use paid ads?

Yes, with prior written approval. Email us your ad strategy and creative before launching. Bidding on our brand name ("The Profit Factory", "theprofitfactory.ai") in paid search is not permitted.

Do you provide marketing materials?

Yes. Once approved, we send you product descriptions, key benefit bullets, suggested social captions, and email copy you can adapt for every channel.

Is there a minimum payout?

No minimum. A single Starter Pack sale earns you €14.10 and will be paid on the next payout date. There is no threshold you need to hit first.

How do I track my sales?

You'll receive a personal dashboard link where you can see clicks, conversions, and earnings in real time. You can also request a monthly earnings report by email.

Is there a vetting process?

Yes. We review every application to make sure your audience is a good fit and that your planned promotion approach aligns with our brand guidelines. Most legitimate applicants are approved within 24 hours.
